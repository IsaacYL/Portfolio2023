Workshop Arcade

Pfizer Gun is a game made with Arduino during the project Arcade with :

Ayoub Zarafa, Steeve Dossou, Jade Gagnepain, Lison Guignard and I.

It is a 1 VS 1 game between the mad scienfic who tried to cure the Covid-19 and the contaminated population who try to dodge it.

Scenario

Dr Raoult tell his story at the two players and the game rules.

The players had to choose : Helping the mad scientist or escape the cure.

The doctor give them badges who represent their roles.

The player access at the scene, head to head.

The game start when the players push the button.

The timer starts. The doctor's assistant have to shot the people before the timer is up with his laser injection.

The player who protect the population can move them to the right and left. He also have two buttons, one who can raise a wall and the other who made them invicible.

At the middle of the time, the survivors mutate, they have to be shot two time.

At the end, the players can leave with proud for defending their ideas.
