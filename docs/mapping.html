Workshop Mapping

This installation was made on Touch Designer during the project Mapping with :

Bastien Denecheau, Mathis Girard, Jade Gagnepain, Lison Guignard and I.

It is a short concert of Disclosure with the song "You and Me".

Disclosure

The group have an artistic direction focus on human physical relationship.

The thin drawing of a face became their signature, appearing on album covers, Instagram photos, posters, etc.

This two DJs love elaborate and lighting stages.

Emotion

You and Me is a video clip, where we see a young couple, with short haircuts and shirtless, portrayed intertwined with their mouths kissing throughout the entire song. It's slow, beautiful, elegant, and hot, gradually building up and making us shift, with shortness of breath, into a pink and warm ocean of desire.

Live Set

Disclosure love pink and purple lighting colors.

They want to encourage human contact and bring a warm atmosphere to the public.
