Kaiso is a multidisciplinary studio. The diversity of the profiles of our designers allows us to vary our activity.
Thus, our creations range from traditional graphics to 3D modeling, including games and motion design.
Our goal is to solve as many needs as possible, using various modes of expression.

We decided to create Kaiso Studio to allow us to improve in our specifications and to learn from the others.

We like to work in a friendly atmosphere and our productivity increase.
